ABOUT

Trans.serve is a family-owned business founded back in 2000. Our company specializes in providing customized solutions for road transporting.
We provide flexible point-to-point service, delivering small loads over short to long distances via various geographic areas. Specializing in transports, we offer all kinds of transports - from single cartons to complete factory relocations. Our aim is to deliver quality transport services.
With offices in five countries and a network of agents we are always close to the client and follow the developments in our markets closely.
